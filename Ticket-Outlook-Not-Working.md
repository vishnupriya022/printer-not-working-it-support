## Issue
User reported that Microsoft Outlook was not sending or receiving emails.

## Environment
- Operating System: Windows 10
- Application: Microsoft Outlook

## Troubleshooting Steps
1. Checked internet connectivity.
2. Verified Outlook account settings.
3. Repaired Outlook account.
4. Restarted Outlook application.
5. Tested send and receive functionality.

## Root Cause
Outlook account configuration issue.

## Resolution
Outlook account was repaired and email synchronization restored.
